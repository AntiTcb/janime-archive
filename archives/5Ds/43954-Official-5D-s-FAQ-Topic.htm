    Official 5D's FAQ Topic

    The official 5D’s FAQ Topic~

    I realized that this forum is becoming cluttered with redundant questions that could be answered easily. Therefore I have composed a hopefully decent guide for others :3

    Series Questions:

    Q: What does 5D’s mean?

    A: 5D’s = Five Dragons that serve the Crimson Dragon. This is confirmed in episode 98.

    Q: How long is it and when is it going to end?

    It is confirmed to last 154 episodes and end in March 2011 so that Zexal can start the next month.

    Q: What time does this take place in the YGO timeline?

    A: Its unknown exactly how into the future 5D’s is, its assumed to be somewhere between 17-20 something years after GX occurred as Ushio is the same Ushio from DM’s period.

    Q: Wait- DM’s Ushio = 5D’s Ushio?

    A: Yes, the 10 anniversary animation book confirms this.

    Q: Does anybody else from DM/GX’s time appear there?

    A: No, but there are numerous references to characters hidden as easter eggs.

    Q: Where can I find subs and raws of this series?

    A: I would check the thread in the media streaming section first for subbed episodes. For raws, I would check on Barchela or Saymove.

    Q: How do I watch 5D’s live when it airs?

    A: First you need to download Keyhole and install it, it allows free streaming of Japanese television. Then you need to look up when 5D’s airs in your timezone (it airs on Tv Tokyo at 6pm in Japan time). For instance, those on the East Coast of America will have it air at 5am for March through October and 4am for October through February because Japan does not observe Daylight Savings.

    Q: The episode just aired today! When is it going to be uploaded/subbed online?

    A: Very soon, within usually hours of its airing a raw appears on Youtube or Saymove. Subs are provided by Goldenumi or yugiohgxst or somebody else within a few days.

    Q: Where can I find pics of Character X?

    A: Oh, there’s several places to look!

    Janime Image Gallery: Outdated, but sorts by character

    Spinel’s Capping Photobucket

    Kaiba’s 5D’s Episode Caps Part 1 | Part 2 : Sorts by episode instead of character, but has a shitton of caps

    Evil Bakura’s Photobucket: Sparse, but has caps from 2chan threads

    Q: Where can I find those huge caps posted right after an episode airs?

    A: Those are posted in a section on 2chan. Be careful though, the caps will only be online for approx 48 hours before they are taken off the server.

    Q: Who or what is Bruno?

    A: He's one mysterious guy with a case of amnesia who is on Team 5D's

    Spoiler: Spoilers
    He was first a human in ZONE's timeline and his friend. But he died due to old age and was rebuilt as an andriod and sent into canon's current timeline to teach Yusei Clear Mind in order to complete the circuit.


    Q: Who or what is the Yliaster Trio?

    A Trio of men who play anti synchro monsters who want to complete a circuit.

    Spoiler: Also spoilers
    The Yliaster Trio was originally one guy called Aporia who suffered three points of despair in his life in ZONE's timeline. His parents, his lover and everybody else. Like Bruno, he was rebuilt as an andriod but instead split into three of them to go along with those three points of despair. And he can combine to form a one winged version of himself. Yeah.


    Q: Is Rua a half signer?

    A: This was a popular rumor back in seasons 1~2. However...
    Spoiler: you gotta love the spoiler tag

    Rua did finally become a full fledged Signer in the Ark Cradle arc however.


    Q: Who is that mystery man from Rudger's flashback?

    A: One of Yliaster's low ranking mooks.

    Q: What is Black Feather Dragon? The fifth or sixth dragon?

    A: It is confirmed to a Signer dragon. Perhaps the sixth since Life Stream did appear first.

    Q: What are the names of the OP/EDs so far?

    A: Kizuna | Start! | Episodes 1-26
    Last Train – New Morning | Cross Game | Episodes 27-64
    FREEDOM | Ozone | Episodes 65-103
    Believe in Nexus | Close to You | Episodes 104-129
    Road to Tomorrow ~Going my Way!!~ | Future Colors | Episodes 130-154


    Q: How are the seasons structured?

    A: According to the 10th anniversary animation book, this is how they are listed:

    Fortune Cup
    Episodes 001-026 (26 episodes)

    Dark Signers
    Episodes 027-064 (38 episodes)

    WRGP (World Riding Grand Prix)
    Episodes 065-137 (62 episodes)

    Ark Cradle
    Episodes 138-154 (16 episodes)

    Q: Wtf is up with the robot shrimp/robofetus?

    A: There's not much I can say without spoiling a twist revealed at the end of the series but!

    Spoiler: major endgame spoilers
    Its Yusei Fudo from an alternate timeline where the world was destoryed and he became old, wrinkly and took on the name of ZONE.


    Q: Why does some people type Jack's name as JACK ATLAS?

    A: Because he's awesome yes. Seriously, I believe the origins stem from an early scan where Jack was first mentioned and Horoko decided to have fun with the translation and spell Jack's name in all caps. The name stuck thanks to Jack's large ham attitude.

    Q: How bad/good is the 5D's dub?

    A: Well first, your mileage is going to vary as always, but the 5D's dub (the English one, I know very little of other dubs) is essentially a watered down version of the original Japanese. Music is completely replaced, dialogue is toned down and sometimes added when the characters are silent, almost everybody gets a name change (some are more questionable then others, Trudge I'm looking at you) voice actors choices are also questionable (as well certain accents) and anything deemed too objectable for children's television is cut or altered. A rather humorous example is Himuro's knife is cut and replaced with dirty magazines.

    You can find a more in depth look at the differences here.

    Q: How can I own this series legally?

    A: Unfortunately, the only way to purchase this series for those who live outside of Japan is with Duel Boxes. Nice, but expensive box sets that cost around 180-220 dollars a pop plus you need a DVD player that can play region 2 DVDs. But if you are interested in buying it anyway, I suggest looking on Amazon or YesAsia for them.

    Q: Where can I find Duel Box scans?

    A: Cindy has been very kind and scanned the first three boxes for our viewing pleasure here. But the other Duel Boxes have yet to be scanned at this time.

    Q: What's the differences between the 5D's manga and anime?

    A: The 5D's manga is essentially an alternate universe of the anime, while the characters do retain their names, faces, their personalities to an extent, and a resemblance to their anime backgrounds, so far a different plot has been unfolding.

    Q: Has the manga been scanned/translated/brought over to the West?

    A: Yes! Scanlations are done by Siliva and are placed in the Trophy Room and moved into the Download forum after a few weeks. New chapters are being published by American Shonen Jump and the first volume will be coming out in a couple of months to the states.

    Q: Why does everybody spell names differently?

    A: It's mainly because Japanese can be romanized/translated in different ways and the dub name changes. I've seen Carly's names come up as Carley/Carlie/Kari. As long as you can tell what the hell they are talking about, there should be no problem.

    Q: What does (name)shipping mean?

    A: This is how pairings are called within the YGO fandom as a whole. The name is to symbolize, relate or just puns at the pairing itself. For instance, Scoopshipping refers to the Jack/Carly pairing as they met because Carly at first wanted to get a scoop on Jack. Some shippings may have multiple names, the Yusei/Kiryu pairing has been coined as Inverseshipping, Treasonshipping and KirYuushipping. A full list of shippings lives here.

    Q: Are Rally/Torunka/Jeager/Luciano female?

    A: No.

    A: The OVA - Evolving Duel! Stardust vs Red Demon's is not canonical. It was simply a bonus episode thrown in to advertise the Buster/Assault Modes of Stardust and Red Demons.

    I'd like to note a little thing on the season structure, in case anyone may be confused. The 10th Anniversary Animation Book came out in 2010 January and only goes up to the Crashtown Arc. The WRGP Arc is listed as 065-present, so going by the Book's information alone, we do not know how it is (officially) structured post-WRGP.

    However, from what I have seen, what was assumed to be multiple arcs during Season 3 of GX, the Animation Book confirmed it as one huge arc called Dimension World. Additionally, the Book considered the Battle City and the post Noa Arc Battle City as the same arc. Therefore, it would follow that everything pre-WRGP, the WRGP itself, and the finale would be considered the same arc, from Episode 065 to 137.

    Going by the Arc naming conventions in the Book, arcs seem to be mostly named after an event (e.g., a tournament, such as Duelist Kingdom or Fortune Cup) or a notable character(s) of that arc. (e.g., Noa, Darkness) Sometimes organizations were used. (e.g., Doma, Dark Signers, The Society of Light) We do not have an official name for the last arc, which could be logically named Ark Cradle, given the location it takes place on, or I could see it named ZONE as well. Unfortunately as far as I know, the arc listings are not given out on the official sites at all (NAS' site or TV Tokyo's 5D's site), making this a case of All There In The Manual.

    Also, you might want to spoiler tag major spoilers such as the Emperor Trio being Aporia or Bruno's true identity. You could try doing it like I did for Paradox in the movie FAQ. Write up a simple, non-spoiler answer explaining who the characters are (e.g., Bruno being a mechanic who aids Team 5D's, etc.), while spoiler tagging major revelations (i.e., the Emperors being Aporia, Aporia's backstory, or Bruno being Antinomy and his backstory).
