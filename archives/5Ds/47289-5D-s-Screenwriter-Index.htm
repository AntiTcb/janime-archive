    5D's Screenwriter Index

    Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Screenwriter Index





    « Duel Monsters | GX | 5D's | ZeXal »


    Just as there is a specific animation team working on an episode, there's a specific writer (sometimes more than one) working on the script. Their names are listed as "Script / Screenplay" (脚本 kyakuhon) in the credits for each episode. The 10th Anniversary Animation Book and the episode opening credits also specify the writers responsible for the series layout.

    Upon watching a really awesome / okay / absolutely horrible episode, you might start wondering who penned the script. Or, let's say you are watching an episode and you start wondering why the plot devices or the Tropes are so similar to another episode. Certain writers like to recycle the same concepts, so comparing the episodes' writers may help shed light on the mystery. It also helps avoiding misblaming a certain writer (or writers) for a certain arc, for example, as no arc was written exclusively by only one writer.

    The episodes are listed by arcs as stated in the Animation Book. Where no arc name is available, unofficial (ie. Fanon) arc names may be used. Such arc names are marked by an asterisk (*).

    Every writer is Color Coded For Your Convenience so you can pick them out more easily. I tried to pick colors to be viewable with both forum skins.
