    Originally Posted by petergriffin77
    which leads me to belive that, the Paradox in the movie is the real deal not an android like bruno/dark glass
    That is not possible. We have confirmation from the series director that the future Paradox comes from is 200 years in the future, and we have clearly seen him being old and dying as a human. The only way he is young and kicking again is that he has been remade as a cyborg, just like Aporia and Antinomy. I would guess his cybernetic features are not shown in the movie because that would spoil his connection to Aporia and the group. Besides, Antinomy is pretty human-like as well, sans the Red Eyes Of Doom.

    Alternately, Paradox was meant to be a human all along, and Yoshida just injected him into the story later on, from which we draw that he's been a cyborg like the others all along. However, I find this unlikely as Yoshida stated in the Animation Book interview that Paradox's "true colors" would be revealed in future episodes of 5D's. Meaning that he may have been planning it all along.
