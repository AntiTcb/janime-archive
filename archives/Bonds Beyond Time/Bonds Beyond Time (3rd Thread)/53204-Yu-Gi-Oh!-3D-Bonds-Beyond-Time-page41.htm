    So, I finally got around to watching the English movie today. A friend and I were MSNing loling about it.

    And...I almost cried. It was just...let's just say that in the times where no music played, or the characters didn't talk out of turn, I was so happy I could have lit up the entire room. The conversations got butchered to crap (The roof scene with Jud - NO - Jaden, and Yusei was horrendously altered, just as one example). I also just realized that Jaden's voice really doesn't suit him. If it was slightly higher pitched and didn't sound as forced, it would have been fine. Paradoz's (inside joke my friend and I developed whilst watching; I made up a whole list for her) dub voice wasn't actually that bad. And interestingly enough, some of the music honestly wasn't that bad - though I still prefer the original score.

    There were a few points where I genuinely laughed ("You are one sick puppy!" "Stardust Mirage, OH NO!"), but then again, I suppose nowadays the only purpose of 4Kids dubs is to laugh at them. Overall, the dub version made me cringe, laugh, and cry simultaneously. I'm still trying to remember where my Mind Bleach is.
