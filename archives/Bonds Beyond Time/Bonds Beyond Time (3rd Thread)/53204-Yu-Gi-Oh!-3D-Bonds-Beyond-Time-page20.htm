    Re: Yu-Gi-Oh! 3D Bonds Beyond Time

    These were the effects Ark posted for the anime versions.

    Sin Cyber End Dragon
    10 Stars/DARK/Machine-Type - Effect/4000 ATK/2800 DEF
    This card cannot be Normal Summoned. This card can only be Special Summoned by send 1 "Cyber End Dragon" from your Extra Deck to the Graveyard. During battle between this attacking card and a Defense Position monster whose DEF is lower than the ATK of this card, inflict the difference as Battle Damage to your opponent. If there is no face-up "Sin World" on the field, destroy this card.

    Sin Rainbow Dragon
    10 Stars/DARK/Dragon-Type - Effect/4000 ATK/0 DEF
    This card cannot be Normal Summoned. This card can only be Special Summoned by send 1 "Rainbow Dragon" from your Deck to the Graveyard. If there is no face-up "Sin World" on the field, destroy this card.

    Sin Paradox Dragon
    10 Stars/DARK/Dragon-Type - Effect, Synchro/4000 ATK/4000 DEF
    {"Sin Parallel Gear + 1 "Sin" monster?}
    When this card is Synchro Summoned, you can Special Summon 1 Dragon-Type Synchro Monster from your Graveyard, ignoring the Summoning Conditions. Monsters your opponent controls lose ATK equal to the ATK of the Synchro Monster Special Summoned by this card's effect while that monster remains on your side of the field. {If there is no face-up "Sin World" on the field, destroy this card.}

    Sin Truth Dragon
    10 Stars/DARK/Dragon-Type - Effect/5000 ATK/5000 DEF
    This card cannot be Normal Summoned or Set. This card cannot be Special Summoned except by the effect of "Sin Paradigm Shift". When a "Sin" monster destroys an opponent's monster by battle, destroy all other face-up monsters your opponent controls and inflict 800 damage to your opponent for each monster destroyed by this effect. If there is no face-up "Sin World" on the field, destroy this card.

    Looking at the card version of the movie!Sinbow, from what I can see it appears to retain the original Rainbow Dragon's effects. (e.g., remove Gem Beasts from the graveyard to increase the card's ATK by 1000 points for each Gem Beast removed.) Similarly to how Sin Cyber End "inherited" Cyber End Dragon's Piercing effect.
