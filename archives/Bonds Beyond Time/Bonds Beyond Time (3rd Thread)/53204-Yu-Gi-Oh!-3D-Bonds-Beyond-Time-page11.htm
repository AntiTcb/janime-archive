    I saw the Yu-Gi-Oh 3D Bonds Beyond Time Today! And I thought for those who are planning to see it, or

    Review:
    If you want a quick synopsis without spoilers, the movie is a good fan service when they get to the duel but before the duel the plot seems a bit rushed and the 3D works well, not the best but it works. Also cool to hear classic(4kids dubbed) and new music.

    Recap and Full Review:
    (NOTE quotes are not exact and I have terrible grammar so if I make a mistake, I'll fix it as soon has possible)
    With the logo popped up, the first 10 minutes of the movie is RECAP of all the previous series. A movie targeting for the fans and they have to do a recap why? But what's interesting about the moment that the narrator is Paradox(The antagonist) but they don't say it's him. By the way the voice of Paradox is awesome!

    With the logo popped up again, we see Jaden running through the streets of Vince, running away from a few dragons. Jaden(Cool to hear Jaden's voice actor again) remarking how awesome this it, when an UNEDITED version of Yubel with a different voice actor and Banner with a different voice actor(Or the actors forget how to do their voices) marked how serious this is. Then Paradox unleashed Stardust Dragon at Jaden.

    The next scenes show a REANIMATED version of New Domino's destruction(Yusie escaping and people dying). But it was a dream of Yusei's whom worries about the sins that his father did onto the city. With him mopping, Jack and Crow with a different voice actor come and try to help Yusei out with a duel. Then Paradox shows up and steals Yusei's Stardust Dragon and say's "Your era shall vanish".

    At their house, the three try to figure at who Paradox was and what he wants. Then Leo, Luna, and Aki show up, showing the three records of the past. Their Jack remarks about Yu-Gi, "Even though his hair outdated..." One record shows the what was happening in the begging of the movie.

    Then the city disappear into ashes. Realizing that Paradox his a time traveler, they got to stop him. So the Crimson Dragon appeared and takes Yusie into the past for the reason of..."I got a feeling".

    So the Crimson Dragon saves Jaden and brings Yusei to Jaden. The two meet and Jaden says that he was after Paradox since he got word from his friends that their cards we're stolen. So Jaden was after Paradox and not the other way around. After that they discovered that Paradox's next attack is Pegasus(Through Jaden's laptop). By the way, their introduction is about the same as a fanfiction.

    The next scene shows Yu-Gi and Grandpa walking around the city plaza. When Pegasus arrives, saying "I'm honored to meet all of you today but in reality it's an honor for all of you to meet me."; Paradox attacks the crowd. With everybody dying and fleeing, Pegasus marks, "This can't be happening, their stealing the show from me." Yu-Gi being the lone survivor, see's Paradox marking how he succeeded. THEN Yu-Gi gets eaten and spit back out by the Crimson Dragon in the past with Yusei and Jaden(Man, the Crimson Dragon does a lot of work in this movie).

    Yu-Gi agrees to help the two and duel Paradox. Before their duel, Paradox states how the future will be destroyed by duel monster's(Want to know details, watch 5d's). With the three saying that the future can be changed, they duel. I'm not gonna mark every single thing in the duel but I will mark some moments I thought we're funny.

    -When Yu-Gi pep talk Yusei in how he's not a failure, Paradox remarked, "Out of all your speechs, that one was your weakest."

    -When Yu-Gi added Dark Magician to his hand, Paradox said, "That was obvious"

    -When Yusei started to get his courage back, he sees the entire cast of 5d's in front of him(Including Sherry which makes the time line of this movie a bit confusing especially if how couldn't Yusei connect the cause of what Paradox is doing with Yialister?) and also see Aki wink at Yusei.

    -The effect of Malefic Truth Dragon change in the dubbed version of the movie, instead of dealing 1000 points of damage it's 800.

    -Banner is the cheerleader in this duel.

    -All of ace monsters of the trio, attack and destroy Paradox.

    After the duel, the trio stated that they should do this again but duel each other instead of saving the world. Then the last minute of the movie is seen with Yusie heading back

    Just came back from the movie, and I got to say, I was impressed. 4Kids didn't do half-bad this time around, and the mandatory jokes were pretty funny. These were the more memorable lines which got the theater laughing.

    "My worst fear has come true! I've been upstaged!" Pegasus during Paradox's attack.
    "He doesn't look so tough. Then again, what do you expect with that outdated hair?"- JACK ATLUS talking about Yugi, probably got the exact line wrong because the theater was cracking up.
    "Yeah, we're from the future, big whoop."-Jaden explaining the situation to Yugi. Jaden was pretty much the jokester here. I can't say much about him, because honestly, I haven't watched much of GX.
    And of course,
    Jaden: I've seen some big monsters, but this one takes the cake.
    Yusei: Yeah, and probably ate it too!-The two of them reacting to Malefic Truth Dragon.

    A few other comments:
    -Pegasus seems to have an ego to match Kaiba now
    -Holy crap! DM and DMG had spoken lines!
    -Its nice to hear Dan Green again, I grew up with him voicing Yugi and Yami Yugi
    -Of course, each character throws out their respective catch phrase at some point
    -The recap was only ten minutes!? It felt like a lot longer then that to me! It did have some scenes from the OPs not shown in the dub though
    -When Stardust Dragon's attack became 10,000, the theater broke into applause. I'm not kidding. My theater loved the movie and also applauded after the end.
