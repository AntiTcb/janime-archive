    Re: Yu-Gi-Oh! 3D Bonds Beyond Time

    Let's see if I can remember....
    Spoiler: The Beginning
    Jaden: *dodging attacks from Rainbow Dragon, jumping through rooftops then landing on the floor* Phew, man they were some close calls! *RD homes in* Uh oh, and I think they're about to get closer! *summons Neos* How 'bout some little help, Neos! *Neos blocks the attack*
    Paradox: *standing over a roof with a laugh* Jaden Yuki. The duelist who never gives up and remains positive until the very end, and the ability to see spirits.
    Jaden: Well, glad to hear my reputation proceeds me!
    Banner: Becareful Jaden, there's something very different about this man.
    Yubel: Agreed, he isn't like anyone I've heard of.
    Jaden: You think so?
    Paradox: Now, to end this *Stardust Dragon appears*
    Jaden: That can't be good!


    *skips to 5D's era, Yusei wakes up from having the dream on Zero Reverse (which btw, how Yusei was sent was the same way but just with belts, and shows cameo on Dr. Fudo) and stands at a view, looking at New Domino*
    Jack: Something the matter, Yusei?
    Crow: Having that dream again, right?
    Yusei: Yeah.
    Crow: Gotta let go of that man, wasn't your dad's fault for his projects being derailed.
    Yusei: I guess, but I just want to make sure this city is still kept safe along with the people living in it. *his helmet is tossed to him*
    Jack: I know what will cheer ya up, mate.
    Crow: How about it, Yusei?
    Yusei: All right, let's do it.

    *the three ride off into the duel lanes*
    Yusei: *in thought* Because of Zero Reverse, New Domino was seperated. But now, it's connected, and everyone is at peace. My dad was blamed for what had happened years ago but I'll make sure to keep everything safe and protected.
    *Paradox starts rolling up from behind*
    Crow: Whoa, check out that Duel Runner!
    Jack: Never seen that person before. I can't believe he's actually challenging us!
    Yusei: I'll take it from here, guys.
    Crow: Go get' em, Yusei!
    *DUEL MODE ENGAGED*
    Yusei: All right, here we go!!!

    *YGO3D Bonds Beyond Time title appears in glorious 3D*

    Re: Yu-Gi-Oh! 3D Bonds Beyond Time

    Originally Posted by Cohen
    I guarantee that you won't see the fanart.
    Then how come my theater showed them?

    Just came back. The movie was really good. The dub was better than I expected.
    The fanart was shown at the very beginning before the 10 minute recap. The one I remember was one in second place which looked really realistic.
    The 10 minute recap was cropped and upscaled to HD and converted to 3D. The 3D in the recap wasn't all that bad, but some parts of DM and GX looked awful due to the upscaling.

    The movie looked amazing. The animation was top-notch. Some dialog made me laugh a bit (or cringe a bit.)

    Jaden: I've seen a bunch a huge monsters, but this behemoth takes the cake!
    Yusei: Yeah, and it probably ate it too.

    (Pegasus heading towards Domino City {With the Millennium Eye? Must be a fake.)
    (Duel ends. Clouds around plaza completely vanish.)
    Pilot: Mr. Pegasus, the clouds have cleared. (and this made the whole theater crack up.)

    Pegasus: It's a dream come true to have all you people around me (or something to that effect.)

    The ending credits played the original Yu-Gi-Oh! theme along with Get your Game On and Hyper Drive. It showed all of the combos the protagonists used in the duel.

    Overall: Very good dub. Paradox sounded very well. The only flaw was the lack of the Japanese score. But the dub score sounded very good and somewhat made up for it. There was even silence when it was needed.

    8.5/10

    Re: Yu-Gi-Oh! 3D Bonds Beyond Time

    I believe the silent moments that I remember were when Yusei, Jack, and Crow went back to their place, trying to figure out what had happened up to where they see Stardust was shown in an article that happened years ago. There was an eerie silence when Yugi got up after Paradox attacked, seeing how everything was destroyed (basically was saying to himself "No...no...NOOOOOOOOO!!") up to where the Crimson Dragon made its appearance and took him. And I'd say the longest silence in the movie was before Yugi's turn as Yusei told him that it's perfectly fine to destroy Stardust if he had to, knowing Stardust would want it that way rather than being under Paradox's control which was very cool because afterwards it had dramatic shots of Yusei's eyes and Yugi's eyes (Paradox chimed in "How touching") and the silence went up until after Yugi drew his card.

    The silent moments were used at perfect times, so glad not constant music and the music used were kinda new and you'd hear familiar tracks used in past dubs but more of a remix and stronger so they didn't cheat on using stock music. The music I'm still inlove with was with Yusei remembering his friends, that track gave me chills mainly because I'm a sucker for dramatic choir chorus. I also think they really made sure voice acting was very good. Greg Abbey and Jaden's VAs were at their best, hitting right emotions and pitches.
