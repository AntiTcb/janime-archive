    Re: Yu-Gi-Oh! 10th Anniversary Movie ~Super Fusion! Bonds That Transcend Time~

    All right, time for feedback on WC2010!

    Now, getting there the championship seemed small but apparently its because another event is taking place downstairs of the con center so its understandable. Once I got my badge, I immediately ran to the movie showing.

    As stated as superwarrior, the preview was 20 minutes and in Japanese but had a narrator ing English which sounded like Greg. They showed Paradox's appearance up to after taking Stardust. Its in subtitles, and they use dub terms such as Turbo Duel and all. They kind of translated the Synchro Summon chant differently. "Together, the wishes combine for a shining star. Become the path's light! Synchro Summon! Take flight, Stardust Dragon!" Then showed like one of the first trailers we have seen of the movie with text. Then they showed a turn of Yusei, Judai, and Yugi. Yusei's turn was using Yusei theme 1 I think. Basically using Reincarnation of Hope to summoning. Next showed Judai's turn, which is the same as we have seen in the special OPs and EDs and taking down Sin Cyber End and Sin Rainbow Dragon. Next, Yugi, which was SO nostalgic! And to me it sounded like 'Passionate Duelists' rather than GX's Excited Duelists. It was entertaining as people cheered for their favorite duelist's move (especially Yami no Yugi's). Had all the same music including the main theme of the movie. At the end as they showed cards, they showed the card arts of the new cards like neos Spiral and Black Magic Spiral. Almost similar art. Stardust Mirage had an image of Stardust Dragon in some crystal form. They didn't show much at all on Paradox (yes his name seems to be kept) nor images of his cards.

    Now as for the 3D effect................Amazing. Seriously, its gorgeous, you can see that nothing even in background is like flat or appears to be clomped together with others. Nope, whole thing was seperate in its own layer and not just on background either but the character. Its like the 3D effect gave them dimensions. Its really amazing especially with the duel since of Sin World as I suspected. So it seems to be in March of 2011 as from another I heard April.

    I came kinda late to Greg Abbey's Q&A. Didn't get to hear about how the dub is doing on the new season. I must've missed quite the bit of it. But was able to get some recorded though. http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ek8n3ZoX7ik

    Here are the pictures of the WC. Now, i was going in cosplay of Yusei but the wig kinda messed up due to the wind blowing from the window and I didn't even realize itso left that, knee pads, gloves, and jacket back in the car. Could've taken pics..with D-Wheel..and..Stardust T_T (my gawd they were gorgeous)
