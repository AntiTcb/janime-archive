    The rest of the post is just my odd ramblings. :V

    ~~~

    And yes, we finally, finally saw the origins of Paradox, at last! It was... close enough, with the Aporia bit. Close enough. At least we were right on the relation part!

    I certainly didn't expect to see him as an old man, but damn if it wasn't an interesting flip on the events. Him being with old!Bruno, Zone and Aporia was pretty kickass, too.

    But yes, Paradox being a Mad Scientist definitely shows now. His obsession with the experiments, his lab coat, and all the technology he/Aporia has/had. I wouldn't be surprised if he designed several things in the future, although it's certain they all worked together.



    Comparisons between Aporia and Paradox. (Note, the picture with Sin Paradox Dragon and the Infinity Sign is merely a comparison with the infinity spam and the infinity sign on Sin Paradox Dragon's tail.)
    • I think the bikes with the transformation capabilities and the draconic decorations could be a giveaway.
    • Then there are the similarities with the whole "fuse by the waist" method.
    • Sin Paradox Dragon bears the Infinity sign, which, while very subtle, could be an evidence for his link to the Four.
    • Sin World -> "Shin" World -> New World.
    • Cubic and Sin Truth - both are summoned by a Trap card (at least as far as anime effects are concerned) and both use Monsters from their theme to activate their effects. Sin Truth Dragon's Raigeki effect activates when a Sin Monster destroys an opponent's monster, and it keeps itself alive by removing a Sin Monster from the Graveyard. Cubic, on the other hand, uses the Machine Emperor parts for its effects (as far as I know).
    • The Accel Synchro, which was Zone's invention, seems very similar in terms of looks to Paradox's time travel.
    • Paradox did taunt Yusei with the feeling of DESPAIR, just like Aporia does.

    And following the pattern with Aporia and Bruno, I believe that Paradox is, yes, also a cyborg, to an extent. But just like Bruno, he's probably just as subtle.

    I did find one part odd in the movie, regarding Paradox.


    When Paradox chooses to "sacrifice" his body, his D-Wheel is destroyed, but he seems to release sparks from his waist. Yes, it could be just for dramatic effect, but it looked... off. Odd. Maybe it's the side effect of his fusion - that he chose to unite with a part supernatural, part technological being. Note that the D-Wheel is also destroyed... or rather, replaced?

    The lines on Sin Truth's head upon the fusion also look like Tron Lines. Circuitry. The way his hair is "plugged" into the Dragon, as well.

    Since Paradox discovered the power of Duel Monsters, it would make sense if he continued his research on it. The development of Sin Paradox and Sin Truth Dragon. The power of "Sin". The reason he went on stealing monsters rather than having his own (with the exception of Sin Parallel Gear and the aforementioned Dragons).

    Though, how he became his younger self is a mystery for the time being. We know Aporia was split into three parts to stand by Zone's side, as the last remaining human who was taken in by them. We don't know if Bruno or Paradox had any last wishes prior dying. Bruno is definitely linked with Zone, as they demonstrated in Episode 107. Has Zone decided to recreate his other comrades thus initiate his "all possibilities must be equal" plan? If Bruno gets a backstory expansion, I do hope this matter will be touched on.

    Though, that previous theory I had on Aporia and Paradox representing the new and primal forces, respectively. Paradox went back in the past in order to save the future, the "beginning". On the other hand, Aporia is trying to correct the problem in the [future], or in other words, the present of 5D's. Hrm.

    Oh, and I'll finish off this post with a realization...


    Paradox's mask. The mask itself follows his face - the marks on the left part (mask's left) represent his eyebrow. The marks on the right represent... the monocle.

    The monocle.

    It was there all along. FFFFFFFF-
