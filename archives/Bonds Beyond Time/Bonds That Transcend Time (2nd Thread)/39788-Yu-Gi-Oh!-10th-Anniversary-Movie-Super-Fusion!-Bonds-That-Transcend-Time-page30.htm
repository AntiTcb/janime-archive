    So me and Remaner cracked the main staff lists from the 10th Anniversary Animation Book. Here are the lists for the movie and the three series!

    All names are written in Western name order. The blurbs are not 100% correct.

    ____________

    ~SUPER FUSION! BONDS THAT TRANSCEND TIME~
    A Duel to protect each of their histories
    Now is the time to cross time---
    And meet the legend!*

    Blurb:

    The commemoration of the beginning of the Yu☆Gi☆Oh! anime series broadcast that began 10 years ago, with the airing of the Yu☆Gi☆Oh! compilation movie. The three successive protagonists meet the strongest opponent and fight in a story unveiled to the public!

    MAIN STAFF

    Original Work: Kazuki Takahashi (Shueisha, Weekly Shonen Jump)
    Serialization: Yu☆Gi☆Oh! GX, Yu☆Gi☆Oh! 5D's (Shueisha, V-Jump)
    Director: Kenichi Takeshita
    Editoral Supervision: Katsumi Ono
    Script (Screenplay): Shin Yoshida
    Duel Composition: Masahiro Hikokubo
    Character Design: Takahiro Kagami
    Monster Design: Takahiro Kagami
    Animation Director: Takahiro Kagami
    Art Director: Junichi Higashi
    Color Design: Ayami Monowa
    Photography Director: Hiroaki Edamitsu
    Sound Director: Takuya Hiramitsu
    Animation Work: Gallop
    3D Image Work: Kyu Deck
    3D Director: Kunihito Mita**
    3D Editor: Motohiko Mizutani**
    Work: NAS
    Production: TV Tokyo / NAS

    * The movie has no subtitle on the staff list page, this line was taken from the pages after the staff list.
    ** From AxA Double A (A×A ダブルエー)

    ____________

    YU☆GI☆OH DUEL MONSTERS
    Beginning of the Legend

    Blurb:

    When the "Millennium Puzzle" gets in the hands of a young boy, the curtains of his personal story open. He meets meets many duelists and grows in experience each time, but the moment to find out about his lost memory and real name is near---

    MAIN STAFF
