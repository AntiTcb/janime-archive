    Re: Yu-Gi-Oh! 10th Anniversary Movie ~Super Fusion! Bonds That Transcend Time~

    Just to spice up the thread a little, Ark translated the of the most critical parts of the Movie Dialogue Wiki, namely the part where the three protagonists talk with Paradox.

    パラドックスとの対峙~デュエル開始
    Confrontation with Paradox ~ Start of the Duel

    Wiki Link: http://www37.atwiki.jp/ygo10thmovie/pages/22.html

    Spoiler: Dialogue ~ Translation by Neo Arkadia

    (Paradox, on his D-Wheel, shows up)

    Yugi: You're that guy from a little while ago.
    Paradox: This one too... And with the King of Duelists, we have a complete set.
    Yugi: What kind of a person are you!? Why would you do all of these horrible things!?
    Paradox: Why, you ask? ... Very well... my name is Paradox.
    Yusei: Paradox!
    Paradox: I seek the best possible [timeline/history/universe/reality/PICK YOUR SCIFI LINGO]
    Yusei: The best possible [term here]!?
    Juudai: What you're doing is the destruction of all humanity!!
    Yugi: Are you trying to say it's perfectly fine to sacrifice everyone like it's no big deal!?
    Paradox: Causing destruction? Sacrifice? Even if you consider me something like the devil, insignificant details like this are just things that are affected by my experiment. I'm doing good things, in the end. Even if I did nothing, the world would still be full of contradictions. Environmental disasters, the nonsensical discrimination of one's fellow human beings, this and other idiotic things all make up a laundry list of contradictions that are entirely the result of human activity.
    Yusei: Even if we haven't solved it right now, humankind will eventually solve them!
    Paradox: Your answer is NONSENSE.
    Yusei: But you, you destroyed my hometown, my city.
    Paradox: Oh contrare, I have remade it. I have caused the Satellite and the City to become one again. This is something I think we can both be in 100% agreement is a good thing, Fudo Yusei! I came from a distant future, in which the world of the future is dead and lays in ruin.
    Yusei: The World... that's dead and lays in ruin... the future...?
    Paradox: Correct. If I let history be, the world will be destroyed. I have come here to change that hopeless future.

    (The recollections of Paradox. Image Videos of his Experiments.)

    Paradox: I looked for the best possible reality, going everywhere, preforming every possible experiment you can think of. And as I did, I noticed the strange and mysterious powers of the game of Duel Monsters... It seemed to be something that improved history.......
    Yusei: Our city... No... those who live there... Yugi's Grandfather and Pegasus, the creator of the game, what will happen to them!?
    Paradox: They're silly trivial things that are just parts of the great experiment, no never mind them.
    Juudai: They're trivial parts of your experiment to you!?
    Yugi: Everyone should have a right to live in the future as they want to!
    Yusei: People have the power to change the future to whatever they want it to be! That's what I believe!
    Yusei: Paradox! Duel! I'll settle things with you right here and now!
    Paradox: Good! I'll crush your pitiful beliefs in Duel Monsters!

    (Paradox's Bike transforms)

    Starfish, Owlhead, and Crabman: !!!

    Juudai: I'm pumped at the idea of beating you!
    Yusei: That future of my friends and I... isn't decided by the likes of you!
    Yugi (Dark): My partner, will you let me take over?
    Yugi (Normal): My other self... YES!

    (Transformation sequence for Yugi)

    Yugi: I will not allow such futures where people's lives become nothing more than stepping stones!
    Yusei, Juudai, and Yugi: Bring it on!
    Crab, Owlhead, Starfish, and Kalamari: DUEL!
    Daitokuji: The very future of the world as we know it rests on your futures, meow~

    SCENE END
    Last edited by Arynis : 11/16/10 at 03:15 PM Reason: Forgot a few of Paradox's lines.
