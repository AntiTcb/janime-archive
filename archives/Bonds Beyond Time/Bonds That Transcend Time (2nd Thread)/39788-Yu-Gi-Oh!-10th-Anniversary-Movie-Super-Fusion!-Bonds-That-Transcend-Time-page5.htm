    Paradox's D Wheel transforms into a flying type and he floats up high and the duel starts.

    Yusei says that everything is his fault, and asks Yugi and Judai to let him take the first turn. Judai and Yugi agrees and say: ''Okay Yusei, kick his ass!".
    Yusei activates a spell that sends two monsters from his hand to the grave but let him add a monster to his hand for two turns(kinda blurry, it may be different). Yusei sends Roadrunner and Bolt Hedgehog to the grave and gets Junk Synchron. Summoning Junk Synchron he gets Roadrunner and Hedgehog on his field, and Synchro Summons Junk Gardna. Yugi and Judai are impressed with the new cards from the future. Yusei sets a card and end his turn.
    Paradox adds a Sin Rainbow Dragon to his hand, summons it( Judai's pissed cause Paradox's using Johan's card XD) and attack with Sin CyberEnd. Yusei uses his monster effect changing Cyberend to defence mode. Then Rainbow attacks and Gardna is destroyed, Gardna's effect changes Rainbow to defense. Yusei uses a card that brings back Gardna.
    Now is Judai's turn. His theme begins to play and he uses Fusion. He then fuses Neos and Gardna to summon Neos Knight. Neos Knight gets half of the attack points of the warrior material monster used. He then destroys CyberEnd, Neos Knight can attack twice so he also destroys Rainbow. Judai sets 4 cards and end his turn.
    Paradox summons Sin Stardust and tunes it to summon Sin Paradox Dragon. He then brings back Stardust from the grave with Dragon's effect. As long as Stardust is on the field all of the opponents monsters loses 2500 ATK points. Paradox Dragon attacks Neos Knight, kinda blurry, I think Judai uses hero barrier.
    Yugi's turn. His theme plays and he uses Yusei's card effect to add Black Magician to his hand. He activates Ancient Rules and summon him, Judai gets all excited seeing Black Magician. Then a magic card summons Black Magician Girl. Yugi uses a magic card to get control of Paradox Dragon but Paradox uses a trap that negates all effects against the Dragon then Yugi smiles and say that his real target is Stardust. Now Yusei got Stardust back. Yugi then uses a card that combine the Magicians attack points and destroy Paradox Dragon. But then Paradox uses a trap, when Paradox Dragon is destroyed he pays half of his life to summon SIN Truth Dragon. He then fuses with him and gets all crazy/face deforming effect exactly like Marik. Everyone is amazed by how gigantic the monster is.

    Post 1769 from XYZ Dragon Cannon gives a detailed summary, so I tried what Ed did and put it through a translator. I'll post what I was able to surmise:


    <Paradox>
    Plays Sin World
    Removes Cyber End Dragon from his Extra Deck to summon Sin Cyber End Dragon
    One card face-down
    Life 4000

    <Yusei>
    Plays ???
    Discards 2 cards (Road Runner and Bolt Hedgehog). During his 2nd Standby Phase, the player can add 1 Monster Card from his Deck to his Hand
    Summons Junk Synchron, effect summons Road Runner, Bolt Hedgehog summons itself, tunes all 3 to Synchro Summon Junk Guardna in Defense Mode
    2 cards face-down
    Life 4000

    <Paradox>
    Uses Sin World to get to Sin Rainbow Dragon
    Removes Rainbow Dragon from Deck to summon Sin Rainbow Dragon
    Not sure here, but I think Sin Rainbow Dragon goes to attack Junk Guardna and Junk Guardna activates his effect which switches Sin Rainbow Dragon to Defense Mode, canceling the attack.
    In any case, Sin Cyber End Dragon attacks Junk Guardna and destroys it, inflicting 1400 LP of Piercing damage. Junk Guardna's destruction switched Sin Cyber End Dragon to defense mode.
    However, Yusei activates The Afterglow of a Miracle (or Miracle's Afterglow) to Special Summon Junk Guardna back to the field in Defense Mode.
    Paradox ends with one card face-down.
    Paradox Life 4000
    Hero Life 2600

    <Judai>
    Fuses Neos with Junk Guardna to Fusion Summon Elemental Hero Neos Knight, who can attack twice and his ATK is determined by adding Neos + half of the other warrior used.
    As such, it rises from 2500 to 3200, meaning Junk Guardna has only 1400 ATK.
    Anyway, Judai has Elemental Hero Neos Knight destroy both Sin Rainbow Dragon and Sin Cyber End Dragon
    Paradox appears to play a card here, that allows him to draw 2 cards?
    Judai ends with 4 cards face-down, his entire hand.
    Paradox Life 4000
    Hero Life 2600

    <Paradox>
    Uses Sin World to get Sin Stardust Dragon into his hand
    Removes Stardust Dragon from his Extra Deck to summon Sin Stardust Dragon
    Summons Sin Parallel Gear and tunes it with Sin Stardust Dragon to Synchro Summon Sin Paradox Dragon
    Sin Paradox Dragon's effect summons Stardust Dragon to the field and reduces Elemental Hero Neos Knight's ATK by 2500, presumably Stardust's ATK.
    Elemental Hero Neos Knight's ATK is now 700, and Sin Paradox Dragon attacks.
    Judai activates Hero Barrier to negate Sin Paradox Dragon's attack
    Paradox plays Sin Crow Stream? which destroys Elemental Hero Neos Knight anyway
    Judai responds by playing Elemental Mirage which allows him to summon back Elemental Hero Neos Knight, which goes back to his base 2500 ATK
    Judai ends by placing two cards face-down
    Paradox Life 4000
    Hero Life 2600

    <Yugi>
    Gets Black Magician to his hand by the effect of Yusei's card played earlier
    Plays Ancient Rules to summon Black magician from his hand
    Plays Of the Master and Student (Bond between Teacher and Student) to summon Black Magician Girl
    Plays ??? (??? Magic Gate?) to take control of 1 monster, Stardust Dragon, while he has two Spellcaster-type monsters on the field.
    Yugi somehow combines the attacks (plays a card lol) of the two Magicians (2000 + 2500) to defeat Sin Paradox Dragon and deal Paradox 500 LP of damage.
    Paradox responds by paying half of his Life Points to summon Sin Truth Dragon
    Yugi ends by playing 1 card face-down
    Paradox Life 1750
    Hero Life 2600

    <Paradox>
    Unsure here, but Paradox plays a card Sin Trigger Selector? (or Sin ???) to summon both Sin Blue Eyes White Dragon and Sin Red Eyes Black Dragon to the field. (It has something to do with removing Sin Parallel Gear, maybe remove 2 Sin monsters, summon 2 Sin monsters? idk)
    Paradox declares the attack of Sin Red Eyes Black Dragon on Black Magician Girl
    Judai defuses Elemental Hero Neos Knight and brings Elemental Hero Neos and Junk Guardna back in to Defense Mode
    Judai activates the effect of Junk Guardna to switch Sin Red Eyes Black Dragon into Defense Mode, cancelling his attack
    Sin Blue Eyes White Dragon destroys Black Magician, triggering Sin Truth Dragon's effect
    This activates Stardust Dragon's effect and it is sent to the Graveyard
    However, Paradox activates Sin Truth Dragon's effect, removing Sin Rainbow Dragon to negate the destruction effect of Stardust Dragon
    Paradox continues by having Sin Truth Dragon destroy the Defense Position Elemental Hero Neos, activating his effect to destroy Black Magician Girl and Junk Guardna as well. 800 LP of damage are inflicted for each monster destroyed this way. (At least that's what I think)
    Paradox plays Sin Cross ???, a revival-type card to summon Sin Stardust Dragon until the End Phase and declares a direct attack
    Judai plays Flute of Summoning Kuriboh to have Yugi place a Kuriboh in his Hand from his Deck, negating the battle damage.
    At the End Phase, Sin Stardust is destroyed and Stardust Dragon comes back to the field
    Paradox Life 1750
    Hero Life 500

    <Yusei>
    Plays Stardust ??? (Stardust Mirage) to summon back the destroyed Black Magician Girl, Black Magician, Elemental Hero Neos, and Junk Guardna
    Judai reveals a face-down ??? (Neos Spiral Force?) to double Stardust's ATK to 5000 when there is a face up Elemental Hero Neos.
    Yugi reveals a face-down ??? (Black Spiral Force) to double Stardust's ATK again to 10000 when there is face-up Black Magician
    Yusei declares the attack of Stardust Dragon at Sin Truth Dragon, destroying it and ending the duel
    Paradox Life 0

    A part of me still likes to think that Paradox's future was destroyed by Duel Monsters indirectly (humanity's sins tainting the cards black, thus leading the world down on the path of ruin).     ...Or you could interpret it as Paradox thinking he was the only person remaining, but he was isolated from other groups of survivors. Just like 2300 A.D. in Chrono Trigger - people lived in separate domes across the Earth, separated from each other by monster infested ruins. One of the domes sent a guy to look for other people, but he never came back (possibly killed). They learned about each others' existence only when Crono and his friends explored the bad future.

    Perhaps Paradox got isolated in an unlucky way - he was alone, far away from other people. Majority of the humanity could have still been wiped out, with little people remaining here and there. It's kind of a staple for most post apocalyptic works, actually.

    Then there's the tagline - "Orders to kill the Creator of Duel Monsters". This would imply someone was out there and indeed gave Paradox the instructions. But that would make Paradox's statement about him being the only person remaining false. I'm surprised we didn't hear anything about that bit. Or it could be possible the trailer lied, or it was merely an early idea. The tagline existed back in the Jump Festa trailer, which was back in October, although the staff diary already showed us the printed storyboard at the end of October.     And looking at it, Paradox did kill Pegasus, but it didn't seem to affect the timeline much (as Paradox's changes were already hurting the timeline anyway), or at least not as critically as I thought, which is a bummer.

    However, killing DM!Pegasus would also lead up to a paradox, even if it's not as critical as pre-DM!Pegasus' death. He's possibly post-Duelist Kingdom Pegasus here, as the Yugi holding Sugoroku's headband already owns a Duel Disk and he has chains for the Puzzle. Either way, if Pegasus dies during DM, he wouldn't be there to create Rainbow Dragon for Johan in GX, which means Judai and the rest of the academia are likely stuck in the Sand World dimension. And thus no one would defend the world from Darkness (Judai wouldn't be there to fight him), which means everyone would vanish, including Paradox, thus he wouldn't be able to travel back in time and kill Pegasus... Grandfather Paradox either way.

    Or perhaps the paradox isn't addressed at this point, or it's gradually tearing reality apart (Delayed Ripple Effect).



    The special could be anything, like interviews with the VAs. (Speaking of which, the producers still weren't revealed officially... I thought they would. Dang.)
    Yeah, the major clue seems to be Paradox being the sole human being left on the planet, who coincidentally, has the power to time travel. There's no reason for that, along with no reason for why the calamity struck his timeline. We only know that it was caused by Duel Monsters.

    Now what is Paradox doing in the movie? He's destroying the future of YGO by going back into the past and destroying it with....Duel Monsters. The irony and paradoxical nature of Paradox's actions are way too explicit to be coincidence. Him being the sole man alive in the future, and then traveling back in past, causing the destruction of the future and present is too much to ignore.

    Its possible that there are other people alive, but just by the facts of the movie alone, we're led to believe he's just the only person. And that's just terribly sad. It explains how Paradox is such a tragic character who's only trying to save his era, but is also the villain, and what the "paradox" of the story is.

    The reason why Paradox's killing of Pegasus did nothing (at least in terms of the events of the movie) was because of the miraculous intervention that allowed for the timeline to be saved from its destiny of the destruction. The Crimson Dragon. Concievably, nothing should've stopped Paradox from fullfilling the timeline paradox and destroying the future. But thanks to the Crimson Dragon and the heroes, they were able to seize the critical moment in "time and space" and defeat Paradox, thus stopping the destruction of the future.

    In terms of Paradox's time, him succeeding in killing Pegasus and destroying the past, killing Pegasus, and destroying Duel Monsters with the powers of duel monsters, would create a cataclysm on the planet and result in the timeline-dimensional distortion/meltdown that results in everyone dying, the planet being destroyed, and him being the only person left alive. Because Paradox has to survive, because he has to go back into the past and try to destroy duel monsters, which leaves him the sole survivor of his time...because he has to go back into the past and try to destroy duel monsters..etc.

    Time line paradoxes are like glitches. They don't really make sense. They loop and are literal anomalies that result in either the complete meltdown of timeline consistency, unless they're completely destroyed at the moment of their inception. Which is why the heroes have to be there in the first place. XD
